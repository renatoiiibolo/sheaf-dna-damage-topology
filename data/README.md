## Data provenance

The feature matrix was derived from TOPAS-nBio Monte Carlo simulations of
cell nucleus irradiation:

- **7 particle configurations**: electrons (0.2 keV/µm), proton pSOBP (4.6),
  proton dSOBP (8.1), helium pSOBP (10.0), helium dSOBP (30.0),
  carbon pSOBP (40.9), carbon dSOBP (70.7)
- **7 oxygen levels**: 21.0, 5.0, 2.1, 0.5, 0.1, 0.021, 0.005 % (v/v)
- **50 nuclei per condition** = 2,450 nuclei total

Six hypoxic DSB populations were derived from the normoxic TOPAS-nBio outputs
using the VOxA oxygen fixation model (Project [1],
[arXiv:2605.12558](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.12558)).

Feature extraction (modalities m1–m7, 107 features) follows the protocol
of the companion study (Project [2], submitted to PMB).
All modality definitions and feature indices are given in `config.py` and
`data/feature_metadata.json`.

## Cross-modality Pearson correlation matrix

The 7 × 7 cross-modality Pearson correlation matrix referenced throughout
the manuscript (r = −0.981 to r = +0.752) is computed from
**condition-level means** in the companion study (Project [2]).
It is used as the independent variable in the per-edge geometric validation
(Fig. 3 of the manuscript) but is not recomputed here.
The companion study repository will be linked when publicly released.
